For my Kanmani
Dear Kanmani,

Happy 3 months anniversary Maria Kutty! I cannot believe its already been 3 months since that first message! I remember being shy sending that first message, that first hello, not knowing how it will turn out.

Pakshe, more than that I remember asking god when I saw your profile first, "Daivamay ee penn kochinay ennik thane theranamay". God definitely heard that prayer and he brought the most beautiful angel into my life! I always am thanking god for bringing you into my life.

It was amazing how I knew within the first few days itself that you were the one. The first thing I fell for, of course, was that beautiful smile of yours. Aa pal kanich ola chiri, ente mole I feel I never can stop loving that smile.

Then within a few days I got to understand the amazing person behind that smile. I felt a special comfort when I was talking to you over phone and that comfort only increased once we met in person!

Ninte kude I can be my true self and loving you is the easiest thing I can do. You have become so precious for me and I will try my best to always keep you happy forever.

You already know I spend most of the day just looking at our photos together and am always dreaming of our future together. There is going to be a lot of ups and downs, atha jeevitham, but I will be there to bring us back together after any fights. Kurach santoshangal, penakangal and cringe ilada pinne ennada jeevitham!

I cannot wait to propose to you in ... days and show you to the whole world. Only ... days left to our marriage kanmani and then we will be together forever! Looking forward to waking up beside you every day, making you breakfast in bed some days, annoying you with my habits, travelling around the world, spending every small moment together and supporting each other in everything!

Happy 3 months, Kanmani.

Love,
Achachen ♡
